About Supreme Overseas

Your Trusted Education Loan Partner

Founded in 2022, Supreme Overseas Educational Loans has helped over 1,500 students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. We understand the financial challenges of international education and have designed loan solutions that make global education accessible.

Our team of financial experts and education consultants work closely with students and parents to create customized loan packages that fit individual needs and circumstances.

Our Services

Comprehensive loan solutions for every stage of your international education journey

Tuition Fee Loans

Cover up to 100% of your tuition fees with competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options starting after course completion.

Living Expense Loans

Get financial support for accommodation, food, transportation and other living costs while you study abroad.

Pre-Admission Loans

Secure funding for application fees, test preparation, and other expenses before you receive your admission letter.

Insurance Coverage

Comprehensive health and travel insurance packages designed specifically for international students.

Currency Exchange

Special forex rates and hedging solutions to protect against currency fluctuations during your study period.

Co-Signer Support

Guidance and solutions for students who need co-signers or alternative collateral options.
